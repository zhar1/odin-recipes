Blueberry Buckle

Description

A vesatile coffee-like cake dessert

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup white sugar

  • 1/4 cup white sugar

  • 1 large egg

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour

  • 2 teaspoons baking powder

  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

  • 1/2 cup milk

  • 2 cups fresh blueberries

Toppings

  • 1/2 cup white sugar

  • 1/3 cup all-purpose flour

  • 1/4 cup butter, softend

  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease an 8x8-inch pan.

  2. Cream sugar, shortening, and egg in a large bowl until light and fluffy.

  3. Stir together flour, baking powder, and salt in a separate bowl.

  4. Add flour mixture to sugar mixture in 2 batches, alternating with milk, beating batter briefly after each addition.

  5. Fold in blueberries. Pour into prepared pan

  6. Make topping: Combine sugar, flour, butter, and cinnamon in a bowl; mix with your hands until crumbly. Sprinkle over cake batter.

  7. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, 25 to 30 minutes.
